Multiplayer Games

Two-player strategy games. Share a link and play from anywhere.

Tic-Tac-Toe
The classic. Take turns placing X and O. First to three in a row wins.
Circuit Breaker
Connect your circuit boards before your opponent connects theirs. Lines can't cross.
Troll Hole
Place numbers 1–9 on a hex board. The Troll eats the empty hole and its neighbors. Highest score wins.
